Instant Pot Beans

Return Home

Description

335 Calories and 20.7g protein per serving.

Two cups of dry black beans cooked in a 6 quart instant pot. Creates Four servings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of dry black beans
  • Enough water to fill up the pot half way

Steps

  1. Put the beans in the pot
  2. Fill up the pot to the halfway line
  3. Press the Beans/Chili button on the Instant Pot
  4. Cook on high for 45 minutes, then let it naturally depressurize
  5. Drain the water
  6. Split into four equal portions
