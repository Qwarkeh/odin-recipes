Instant Pot High Protein Oatmeal

Return Home

Description

503 calories, 38.9g protein

A simple high protein oatmeal recipe that can be made in an Instant Pot.

Ingredients

  • Steel Cut Oats
  • Textured Vegetable Protein (TVP)
  • Spinach
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add 1/2 cup of steel cut oats
  2. Add 1/2 cup of TVP
  3. Add 1 teaspoon of salt
  4. Add 10 leaves of spinach
  5. Add 2 cups of water
  6. Push the porridge button on the instant pot
  7. Cook on high pressure for five minutes
  8. Open pressure valve, stir and serve
