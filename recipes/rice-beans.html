Instant Pot Rice and Beans

Return Home

Description

521 Calories, 37.8g of protein

Rice and beans with added tofu and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 White Rice
  • 1/2 of dry beans that have been cooked (cooked volume is greater than 1/2 cup)
  • 2/3 cups of frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/3 block of extra firm tofu

Steps

  1. Place white rice into the pot
  2. Place the prepared beans into the pot
  3. Add salt
  4. Add the mixed vegetables
  5. Add 1.5 cups of water, mix contents
  6. Push the rice button on the instant pot
  7. Cook for 12 minutes on high
  8. Rapidly release the pressure
  9. Add tofu, mix contents
  10. Let cool and serve
